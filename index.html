My Story

I've been a DJ for over 10 years. When the pandemic hit and nightlife shut down, I all of a sudden had a lot of free time. I've always been into computers, and that's when I stumbled upon coding. It felt like solving puzzles, which I love.

I started by teaching myself and after two months I joined a six-month bootcamp where I learned about the MERN stack. When the bootcamp ended, and things started to open up again, I went back to DJing. I wasn't ready to switch careers just yet. Partly because I felt like I still had a lot to learn in the field of development, but also because I enjoyed following tutorials and learning at my own pace.

Later on, I got a chance to join another bootcamp, this time sponsored by GIZ. It was different; it was more about the basics of computer science. But even after finishing that, I wasn't in a rush to get a job as a web developer. I liked coding as a hobby.

But now, in 2023, things have changed. I moved to the Netherlands, and I feel it's time to switch things up. I want to work as a web developer and keep my DJing as a side passion. I genuinely believe that coding is what I want to do.

What i'm looking for

I would like to be somewhere where I can grow big time. A place with good vibes and a team that does things the right way. It'd be cool to have some seasoned pros around, so I can pick up new tricks and keep leveling up. And although I'm flexible, I prefer working face-to-face. I love being on-site and in the mix with everyone.

AI-Driven Journaling Platform

Description

I dove into this project during the "AI-Powered Fullstack Next.js App" course from Frontend Mentor. Imagine jotting down your daily thoughts, and then, boom, AI jumps in, slaps on a fitting title, colors it based on your mood, and even tells you if it's a happy note or not. Curious if you've been on cloud nine or a bit down lately? Just ask it, "Hey, was I feeling good the past 10 days?" and it'll comb through your entries, giving you a quick mood recap. Plus, you get to see mood swing charts.

technologies used

Next.js - Prisma - PlanetScale - OpenAI API - Langchain - Tailwind
